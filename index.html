About Laxmi Charities

Founded in 1973, Laxmi Charities is a registered non-profit society dedicated to promoting education and providing relief to those in need. There is a strong interest among stakeholders in committing to sustained financial support for the years to come. This ongoing financial backing will enable the organisation to expand its reach and enhance its impact in a sustainable manner.

About Laxmi Charities Scholarship for Academic Excellence

The Laxmi Charities Scholarship for Academic Excellence recognises and rewards students demonstrating outstanding academic performance. This programme supports those who need additional financial assistance to continue their education. The scholarship not only acknowledges exceptional achievements but also provides long-term support through a multi-year funding programme. Once selected, students remain eligible for the scholarship throughout their academic journey, from school to higher education, ensuring consistent financial assistance until they complete their studies. This programme is designed to alleviate financial stress over multiple years of their education, allowing students to focus on their academic and personal growth without worrying about tuition or other educational expenses. Educational impact study by Give Grants* on Laxmi Charities The programme was found to be highly relevant to the socio-economic and geographical needs of beneficiaries in Tamil Nadu, offering hope and confidence to students and their families. The scholarships supported students from low-income families, ensuring continuity in higher education despite financial challenges. The majority of students demonstrated strong academic performance, with the programme playing a key role in sustaining their educational journey. This clearly highlighted the effectiveness and academic necessity of the support provided by Laxmi Charities. The intervention also led to a significant increase in higher education participation in Tamil Nadu, contributing to the state's gross enrolment rate. *Give Grants -They connect corporates with non-profits to maximise their social impact through CSR initiatives.
