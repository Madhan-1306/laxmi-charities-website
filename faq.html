Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Laxmi Charities?

Laxmi Charities is a non-profit organization committed to supporting economically disadvantaged and deserving students through educational scholarships, mentorship, and institutional partnerships.

2. Who can apply for scholarships from Laxmi Charities?

Students from underprivileged backgrounds who demonstrate strong academic merit and commitment are eligible to apply. The applicant must be enrolled in a recognized and reputed school, college, or university.

3. How do students apply for scholarships?

Students can apply through their educational institutions, which are partnered with Laxmi Charities. Institutions are required to verify and forward eligible student applications. (Online application system coming soon.)

4. Which courses or fields of study are eligible?

Support is extended to students across a wide range of fields including: • Engineering • Law • Medicine • Agriculture • Arts & Sciences • Vocational and technical training

5. Is there a deadline to apply for scholarships?

Yes, scholarship applications are usually accepted during a specific window each academic year. Institutions will be notified, and students should check with their school/college for deadlines.

6. Does Laxmi Charities offer support beyond tuition fees?

The scholarship offered by Laxmi Charities is a wholesome grant, intended to provide meaningful financial relief to the student. The amount is disbursed in a flexible manner, allowing the student to use it as needed within key academic expenses such as: • Tuition fees • Books and learning materials • Hostel or accommodation charges • Examination or lab fees • Transportation or other education-related needs We trust our scholars to use the funds responsibly, ensuring that the support contributes directly to their academic growth and success.

7. Can previous beneficiaries apply again?

Yes. Students who received scholarships in the previous year and continue to meet the eligibility criteria may reapply, usually through a simplified renewal process.

8. What is the Laxmi Charities Mentorship Program?

The mentorship program connects students with professionals, alumni, and subject experts who guide them on academics, career development, and life skills.

9. How does Laxmi Charities select students?

Selection is based on a combination of: • Financial need • Academic performance • Institutional recommendations • Interview or verification (if required)

10. Can institutions request to partner with Laxmi Charities?

Yes, educational institutions interested in collaborating can reach out via email to explore partnership opportunities.

11. How are donors and trustees involved?

Our trustees and donors play a key role in funding and guiding the mission. They are also regularly updated on the impact through reports and testimonials.

12. How can one support Laxmi Charities?

You can support us through donations, volunteering, or becoming a mentor. Please contact us at shyamalaa@laxmicharities.in for more details.
