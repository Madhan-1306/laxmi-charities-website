ALUMNI CONNECT

Alumni Connect is a dedicated initiative by Laxmi Charities that brings together former beneficiaries of our educational support programs. Rooted in the principles of gratitude, growth, and service, this platform aims to strengthen ties among alumni, while fostering a culture of giving back. It is a space for reconnection, where shared experiences become the foundation for mentorship, collaboration, and long-term impact. Over the years, Laxmi Charities has supported countless students in their academic journeys. Many of these individuals have gone on to make significant contributions across various fields. One such alumnus is Mr. Lakshminarayan Duraiswamy (Laks), currently Managing Director of Sundaram Home Finance, who reflects: "That scholarship was not just money, it was a quiet nudge from the universe saying, ‘Keep going.’ I never forgot that moment." His story speaks to the profound effect a single act of support can have, and exemplifies the spirit of Alumni Connect. The platform offers alumni opportunities to engage through networking, mentorship, volunteering, and knowledge sharing. It celebrates individual journeys while creating avenues for collective contribution. Whether it's through offering guidance to a student, sharing professional insights, or supporting future initiatives, each member plays a part in carrying the legacy forward. Alumni Connect is not just a network, it’s a living testament to the values of Laxmi Charities. By participating, alumni help create a supportive, intergenerational community that continues to empower lives and uplift futures.

MENTORSHIP PROGRAM

Supporting Scholars Beyond Scholarships

While financial assistance eases the burden of education, true empowerment comes from long-term, holistic support. Our Alumni Mentorship Program is designed to bridge the gap between academic funding and personal development by connecting scholarship recipients with accomplished alumni who serve as mentors. These mentors provide guidance that goes beyond textbooks, helping scholars navigate academic challenges, career decisions, and personal growth.

How It Works

Each scholar is thoughtfully matched with a mentor based on shared interests, academic background, and professional goals. Mentorship can take the form of one-on-one meetings (in-person or virtual), networking sessions, and skill-building workshops. The program is structured but flexible, allowing mentors and mentees to define a rhythm that works for them, whether it’s monthly check-ins, project collaborations, or occasional life advice.

Goals of the Program

Career Readiness: Mentors share insights on industries, career paths, and postgraduate opportunities, helping scholars set realistic goals and build strategic roadmaps. Confidence & Exposure: Through conversations, mock interviews, and sharing personal stories, mentors help scholars step outside their comfort zones and prepare for real-world challenges. Life Skills Development: From communication and leadership to resilience and decision-making, the program fosters personal and professional growth. Community Building: Scholars become part of a larger, value-driven network of alumni who understand their journey and are invested in their success.

Why Alumni Mentorship Matters

Alumni bring lived experience, industry knowledge, and a shared sense of purpose. For scholars, many of whom are first-generation students or navigating unfamiliar systems, having a mentor who has walked a similar path can make all the difference. This program transforms alumni connections into meaningful relationships that inspire, uplift, and empower the next generation.
